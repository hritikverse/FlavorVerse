Indian Style Pasta

Recipe Details

Pasta is a famous Italian recipe made with creamy tomato sauce, since sauces are not easily available
at our markets I have shared a simple version of pasta recipe made with onions, tomatoes and some spices.

Ingredients

2 cup Pasta
2 tbsp Oil
2 cloves Garlic
1 inch Ginger
2 Onion (medium size)
4 Tomatoes (medium size)
1 Carrot
1/2 Capsicum
1 tsp Garam masala
2 tbsp Chili sauce
2 tbsp Tomato ketchup
pinch Pepper powder
Coriander leaves
Salt to taste
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

Step 1) Bring water to a rolling boil and add required salt, then add pasta and cook until its soft and tender, I cooked for
approximately 10 minutes. follow the instructions/time on the packet for best results. Once its done drain the water and keep it
aside.
Step 2) heat oil and add finely chopped garlic, finely chopped ginger and saute it for a few seconds.Then add onion and cook
for another minute.
Step 3) Once the onions are translucent add tomatoes and cook until soft. Always add double the tomatoes over onions to get
that nice tangy taste.
Once the tomatoes are soft add chopped carrot and capsicum and cook for few more minutes. Add water if required.
Step 4) Now add required salt, garam masala powder, tomato ketchup and chili sauce (if you are mot using chili sauce add 1 tsp
of chili powder). Then add the cooked pasta and mix well,heat for 1 more minute. Finally sprinkle some pepper powder
and some chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
Finally grate some Parmesan cheese on the top before serving. That's it our delicious and easy Indian style pasta recipe is
ready to be enjoyed.Please do try this recipe at home and share it with your friends and family. Happy Cooking !!!!
